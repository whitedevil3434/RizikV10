# Social Ledger Redesign - Complete ✅

## Problem Solved
The Social Ledger screen looked like a completely separate app with dark theme colors that didn't match the rest of the Rizik app.

## Solution Applied
Redesigned the entire Social Ledger screen to match the app's beautiful, clean design language:

### Color Scheme Updated
**Before (Dark Theme)**:
- Background: `#0A0E21` (dark blue)
- Cards: `#1D1E33` (dark gray)
- Text: White/Light colors
- Accents: Cyan, bright colors

**After (Light Theme)**:
- Background: `#F8F9FA` (light gray) - matches app
- Cards: White with subtle shadows
- Text: Black87/Gray colors
- Accents: Green[700], Red[700] - professional

### Design Principles Applied

1. **Consistency**: Matches Khata OS and Consumer Home design
2. **Readability**: High contrast, clear typography
3. **Bengali First**: All primary labels in Bengali
4. **Professional**: Subtle shadows, proper spacing
5. **Color Psychology**: 
   - Green = Money owed to you (positive)
   - Red = Money you owe (negative)
   - Light backgrounds for better readability

### Visual Hierarchy
- Primary info: Bold, larger text
- Secondary info: Regular weight, smaller
- Tertiary info: Gray, smallest
- Actions: Colored buttons with clear labels

### Accessibility
- High contrast ratios
- Clear touch targets
- Readable font sizes
- Color + icon coding (not just color)
