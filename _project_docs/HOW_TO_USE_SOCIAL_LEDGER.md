## Settling Debts

### When Someone Pays You Back
1. Tap on the person card
2. Tap **"Mark Received"** button
3. Confirm the amount (auto-filled with balance)
4. Tap **"Confirm"**

**Result**: Balance updates, transaction recorded

### When You Pay Someone Back
1. Tap on the person card
2. Tap **"Pay Back"** button
3. Confirm the amount (auto-filled with balance)
4. Tap **"Confirm"**

**Result**: Balance updates, transaction recorded

## Tips & Best Practices

### 1. Add Transactions Immediately
- Don't wait! Add transactions right after they happen
- Fresh memory = accurate records

### 2. Use Clear Descriptions
- Good: "Lunch at Cafe ABC - 15 Nov"
- Bad: "Food"

### 3. Settle Regularly
- Don't let debts pile up
- Settle small amounts quickly

### 4. Check Before Asking
- Before asking "Did you pay me?", check the app
- Avoid awkward conversations

### 5. Use for All Money Exchanges
- Rent splits
- Grocery shopping
- Taxi fares
- Restaurant bills
- Borrowed money
- Lent money

## Common Questions

### Q: Can I edit a transaction?
A: Currently no, but you can add a correction transaction

### Q: Can I delete a transaction?
A: Not yet, but coming in future updates

### Q: Does this send money?
A: No, it only tracks. You still pay outside the app

### Q: Can I split with multiple people?
A: Currently 1-on-1 only. Group splits coming soon!

### Q: Is my data private?
A: Yes! Stored locally on your device only

### Q: What if I partially pay?
A: Enter the partial amount when settling
